As a student who took Civic Technology with professor Gawel, Civic Technology
is about how the government uses technology to help the general citizenry of 
The United States as well as how well of a job it does when it comes to updates or
Maintenance. Equally so, Civic Technology is about empowering constituents  on how to 
to stay informed of government plans and legislation. Moreover, he mentioned that former 
President Obama had explained the main problems Civic Technology is supposed to 
address and one of them being transparency. Civic Technology is also used for revealing 
public records such as teacher salaries, infrastructure project cuts, politicians public 
spending, healthcare spending etc. It can also contribute to online accountability 
tracking of politicians so voters can keep track of how politicians vote and when
to contact their politicians in case it goes against their voting ideals. This protects 
the foundations of democracy because it avoids politicians to vote with their own self 
interests or switch parties when their voters think a certain way from them. 
Smart cities can benefit from Civic Technology in the way New York City implemented 
a state of the art law enforcement technology system.Former mayor of New York City 
Michael Bloomberg stated, “ It acts as a communications hub, a place where a number 
of our cities advanced computer counter terrorism system can relate information, 
so that it can be analyzed and acted upon.” Moving forward, he later on explained 
how there were a system of cameras which were provided by the private sector 
and these private sector companies which include many industries like finance, 
baking etc- to sound an alarm if there is suspicious activity detected in these
systems. This can be an unattended package in the entrance of a building. 
These types of systems can also be used in sustainability efforts and many more !
